About me

I am a Computer Science student at University of Massachusetts Lowell. I grew up in Bellingham, Massachusetts, during the school year I live off campus in an apartment in Lowell. Before becoming a Computer Science student my only coding experience came learning Labview when I participated in FIRST Robotics in 2017. Although Labview is a visual programming language and greatly limited potential it was the perfect platform for someone with no coding experience to program my robot to do basic autonomous functions. Since then I have learned the C and C++ Languages and hope to expand my knowledge by taking Database 1 and GUI 1 this semester and narrow down what style of development I would like to focus on for my career.
